About Me

Where code meets canvas, and algorithms blend with artistry—that's where you'll find me.

I'm a Software Engineering Technology student with a soul deeply rooted in creative expression. My world exists at the fascinating intersection between logical precision and artistic freedom, where both hemispheres of my brain collaborate to create meaningful digital experiences.
